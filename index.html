Hi, I'm Svjetlana, journalist

I am a graduated journalist and a beginner web developer. I live in Banja Luka, Bosnia and Herzegovina.

From 2009, until 2019 (with minor interruptions) I worked for national television RTRS - as a TV journalist.

I wrote articles for the media and portals: Nezavisne novine, Prosvetni pregled, BUKA, Efm .. I worked on the project CCi and USAID TV show "BAR CODE", on consumer protection in Bosnia. Participated in the realization of the project Media Center Sarajevo.

I graduated at the Faculty of Political Science in Banja Luka - Department of Journalism.

I like to read, write and travel.

"Great journalism will always attract readers. The words, pictures and graphics that are the stuff of journalism have to be brilliantly packaged; they must feed the mind and move the heart." Rupert Murdoch

And future front-end developer

Through the work of a journalist, I learned that certain stages of work are necessary for the story. From recording, text writing to editing. I liked the same thing in programming, the road from an idea to its realization.

From writing HTML, adding CSS, to adding functionality with JavaScript. On the journey through web development, to it becoming my job, there is a long road ahead.

The plan is to write down the learning process on this site to help someone who will start tomorrow as I did. From daily tasks, courses I took, reading literature, to things that took me more time, and things you don't need to waste time with.

Goal: Connect these two passions to work.
